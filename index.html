I graduated Occidental College in 2020. At the FJM Foundation, I work closely with Congressman David Dreier, former chairman of the House Rules Committee, to recruit student journalists to participate in the construction of a memorial on the National Mall commemorating fallen journalists.

At Occidental, I majored in Politics and minored in Economics and Urban & Environmental Policy. I wrote my senior thesis on Israel-related partisanship in the United States Congress. I interviewed nineteen current and former members of Congress as part of my research. I have worked on six political campaigns since the age of thirteen.
